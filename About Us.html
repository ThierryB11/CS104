New Jersey Institute of Technology Public Transportation System

Do you find it difficult to get to class on time? always missing the bus, or the bus doesn't come on time? Well, now NJITPTS will help you arrive on time.

Many college students arrive late to class because of transportation and transportation arrives late to the stops, because of the number of people the buses are picking up. By consolidating transportation, school buses contribute to reducing the number of individual cars on the road during peak traffic times. This can help avoid delays caused by traffic congestion, making the transportation process more time-efficient. The regular and consistent nature of school bus transportation contributes to students developing a routine. This routine, including the timing of bus arrivals and departures, helps students manage their time effectively and arrive at school on time.

NJIT PTS college buses typically follow scheduled routes with designated different pick-up times, so if whatever reason you miss one bus, you can always check the schedule and get on the other one. The college buses only pick up NJIT students. If the college bus arrives late for extreme reasons the college will be informed of that and the professors too, that will help your attendee's grades stay on track.

NJITPTS buses will only pick up NJIT students.
