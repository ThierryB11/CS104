FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


How Much does the NJITPTS ticket cost?

It costs $85.30 per semester, and you can use it in any location detailed on the Tickets tab as often as you need.


Which days do the NJIT buses not function?

The NJIT buses do not function over weekends and some major holidays.


Why should I use NJITs Public Transportation System instead of the regular NJ Transit one?

Since our system operates in a determinate and rather small area, the buses that cycle at the stops are much more likely to get you on time to your destination. It offers a better price in the long term and one differential benefit it has is that you travel among students which makes it your safest option as well.


What happens if I lose my ID? Can I still get on the bus?

For sure, Highlanders are the top priority of our service. Every NJIT bus is well equipped with a touchpad system where you can log in with your institutional email or UCID.


Can I share my NJITPTS monthly pass with other students?

We are concerned about the need to share your pass to help any friend get to campus more easily; however, there is a limit of 10 extra uses that you are assigned each semester. Remember you can only share this pass with NJIT students.
